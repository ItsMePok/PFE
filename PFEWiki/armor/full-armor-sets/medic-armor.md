# Medic Armor

## Medic Armor

Set Effects:

* Health Boost 6 (+12 Hearts)
* Speed 2
* Resistance 2 Set Radius Effects:
* Regeneration 6 to all players within a 10-block radius
* Absorption 6 (+8 Absorption Hearts) to all players within a 10-block radius
