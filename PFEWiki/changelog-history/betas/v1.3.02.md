### Most armor sets now require you to craft them via the [Upgrader](../../blocks/crafters/upgrader.md)

* This also now allows prior enchants, durability, and dynamic properties to be transferred to the new piece of armor
  * Note: The item data will be transferred from the item in the recipe that is listed with a yellow dash (like this: <mark style="color:yellow;">-</mark> \[<mark style="color:green;">1/1</mark>] (<mark style="color:blue;">PFE</mark>) Onyx Helmet)
* The armor sets that this applies to are:
  * [Creedite Armor](../../weapons/swords/amethyst-sword.md)
  * [Onyx Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/onyx-armor.md)
  * [Holy Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/holy-armor.md)
  * [Hellish Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/hellish-armor.md)
  * [Godly Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/godly-armor.md)
  * [Demonic Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/demonic-armor.md)
  * [Medic Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/medic-armor.md)
  * [Molten Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/molten-armor.md)
  * [Galaxy Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/galaxy-armor.md)
  * [Void Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/void-armor.md)
  * [Astral Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/astral-armor.md)
  * [Death Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/death-armor.md)
  * [Nebula Armor](../../armor/full-armor-sets/nebula-armor.md)
* Additionally the [Creedite Sword](../../weapons/swords/amethyst-sword.md) can also be crafted via the Upgrader
