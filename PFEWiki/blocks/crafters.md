# Crafters

## General info:

* Crafter are crafted in the Crafting Table

## Spell Crafter

### Can Craft:

* PFE spells
* Empty Vial
* Spell Pottery sherd
* Magic 8 Ball

## Dye unmixer

* Unmixes all dyes into the dyes it can be crafted from (will give all that were used to craft it back even though it only shows one)
